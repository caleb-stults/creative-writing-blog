# Isolation

*A poem I wrote long ago.*  

Loneliness, so familiar is that   
Numbing pain, almost a comfort.  
But you push on, knowing you  
Have escaped a pain far greater.  

For an icy heart may be plunged into   
The fires of passion to be restored.  
But when loves takes bits of the heart,  
Its gone; utterly, magnificently, destroyed.
